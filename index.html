Interesting facts about Flying Teddy Bears

  • Teddy bears got their name from the story that Teddy Roosevelt refused to shoot a bear cub while on a hunting trip in 1902.
  • The first toy stuffed bear was created by German toymaker Margarete Steiff.
  • Margarete Steiff created a soft plush with movable arms and legs and a friendly face after taking her nephew to the zoo in 1903.

Interesting facts about Camel

  • Camel's ears are furry.
  • Camel can move across the sand easily because of their special designed feets.
  • When they find water, they drink as much they can.

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are the reptiles that are the part of the Iguana Suborder.
  • Changing skin color is an important part of communication among chameleons.
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches.

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  • Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  • Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  • Pigeons have excellent hearing abilities.

Interesting facts about Flying Cicada

  • Male Cicada make the loud whirring or buzzing sound.
  • Most Cicadas have red-orange eyes
  • Cicadas predators, or animals that will eat them, don’t know they’re coming.

Interesting facts about Panda

  • Panda's live in thick Bamboo Forests.
  • They live on a bamboo tree and eat Bamboos also.
  • Wild pandas live about 15-20 years and pandas in human care can live about 30 years.
